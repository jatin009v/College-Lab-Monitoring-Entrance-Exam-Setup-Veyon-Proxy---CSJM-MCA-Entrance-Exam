🛡️ "Kiosk Mode" (Cheating Rokne ka Ultimate Tarika)
Agar aap chahte ho ki student browser ke alawa kuch aur khol hi na paye, na tab change kare aur na hi taskbar dekhe, toh ye command use karo:

Plaintext
msedge.exe --kiosk https://your-exam-link.com --edge-kiosk-type=fullscreen
Isse browser Kiosk Mode mein khulega, jahan Alt + Tab ya Windows Button kaam karna band kar dete hain (kuch had tak). Student ko browser band karne ka option bhi nahi dikhega.
